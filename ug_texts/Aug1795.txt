
Thursday, 17 August 1995

The Council met at 2.30 p.m. in Parliamentary Building, Kampala.

(The Vice Chairman, Al-Haji Moses Kigongo, in the Chair.)


PRAYERS

COMMUNICATION FROM THE CHAIR


THE CHAIRMAN:  Hon. Members, it seems some people are not serious.  We are less than ten people in the House and we cannot proceed.  So, we adjourn until Tuesday, 22nd August 1995.

(The Council rose at 2.35 p.m. and adjourned until Tuesday, 22 August1995.)
