
Tuesday, 11th January, 1994

The Council met at 2.30 p.m. in Parliament House, Kampala.

PRAYERS

(The Vice- Chairman, Al-Haji Moses Kigongo, in the Chair).

The Council was called to order.

    MOTION FOR A RESOLUTION

HON. MEMBER:  Point of information.  Mr. Chairman, in view of the fact that we do not form a quorum, is it in order for us to go on with this business?  

THE CHAIRMAN:  It is not in order, therefore, if we adjourn for 15 minutes please.

(The Council adjourned for 15 minutes)

THE CHAIRMAN:  I am sorry we do not form a quorum, I therefore, adjourn the Council until tomorrow at 2.30 p.m.


(The Council rose at 2.35 p.m. and adjourned until Wednesday 12th January, 1994 at 2.30 p.m.)
