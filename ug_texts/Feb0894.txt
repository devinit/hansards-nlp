Tuesday, 8th February, 1994

The Council met at 2.30 p.m. in Parliament House, Kampala.

PRAYERS 

(The Chairman, Al-Haji Moses Kigongo, in the Chair).

The Council was called to order.

MOTION

(Debate continues)

MR. ADAM IMAGA:  Point of order.  Mr. Chairman, is it in order for us to go on with this important topic when we have no quorum here?

THE CHAIRMAN:  It is not in order, and therefore, we adjourn for 15 minutes.  Order, please.  We have to follow the law, and therefore, I adjourn the Council indefinitely.  Thank you.
