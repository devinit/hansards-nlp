Hon.  Jimmy Odari.  
(Hon. Odari moved a Notice of Motion ) 
RENOVATION/RE CARPETING OF CHANGAMWE NEW FLATS ROAD  
THAT AWARE  that County transport,  including County roads is a mandate of the 
County government as provided under the Fourth Schedule Part Two (5)  (a) of the Constitution 
of the Republic of Kenya;  
FURTHER AWARE  that well maintained roads make a crucial contribution to economic 
development and bring social benefits, for instance, providing a ccess to employment, social 
health and education;  
CONCERNED  that the road adjacent to Changamwe New Flats is poorly maintained thus 
can cause disaster such as road accidents, threatening the lives of drivers, riders and pedestrians;  
I therefore urge this A ssembly to RESOLVE  that: 
The County Government of Mombasa refurbish/recarpet  the road adja cent to the 
aforesaid New Flats in Changamwe Ward.  
Thank you Hon.  Speaker . 
Deputy Speaker  (Hon. Mswabah Rashid) : Thank you very much, yes C lerk.
