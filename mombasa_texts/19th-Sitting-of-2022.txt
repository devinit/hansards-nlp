NOTICE OF MOTION  
ESTABLISHMENT OF YOUTH INTERNSHIP APPRENTICESHIP AND VOLUNTEERING PROGRAMME 
IN THE COUNTY  
Yes, Honourable  Athman Mwamwiri?  
The Leader of Majority (Hon. Mwamwiri ): Thank you Mr. Speaker . Mr. Speaker Sir, I 
wish to give notice of the following Motion:  
THAT AWARE THAT,  Article 55(a) of the Con stitution of Kenya, 2010 requires that the 
State to take measures inclu ding affirmative action programmers to ensure that the youth 
access education and training;  
FURTHER AWARE THAT,  the national youth apprenticeship programme s have  been 
instrumental in im parting useful job skills and knowledge to over 30,000 youth nation -
wide; 
NOTING THAT  through internship, apprenticeship and volunteering the youth gain 
valuable work skills, experience and may hopefully gain employment;  
CONCERNED THAT, Mombasa County face s an ever -growing number of young 
graduates who need technical skills, mentoring and need to be nurtured to take 
management and leadership roles in industry and government in the future but lack places 
to do so;  
I, therefore urge this Assembly to RESOLVE:  
THAT,  the County Government of Mombasa to establish an internship, appre nticeship and 
volunteering programme  to nurture young graduates to gain technical skills, experience 
and to facilitate smooth transition to the labo ur market.
