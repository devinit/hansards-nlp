MOTION  
 
PROHIBITION OF BARS AND NIGHTCLUBS OPERA TING IN RESIDENTIAL AREAS AND 
EDUCATIONAL  INSTITUTIONS  
 
 Yes, I now call upon Hon. Mohamed Hatimy to move the M otion as amended . Yes 
Hon. Mohamed . Mr. Speak er I beg to move the following M otion;  
 
(Hon. Mohamed Hatimy moved)  
 
WHEREAS  bars and night clubs are allowed to operate in the County subject to 
certain conditions and restrictions;  
 
WHEREAS  the operation of these entertainment establishments emit noise pollution 
and have adverse influence to youth s and other residents;  
 
WHEREAS  a recent High Court judgement prohibited the operations of bars and 
night clubs in residential areas and schools;  
 
WHEREAS  if the current state of affairs whereby these entertainments are left 
uncontrolled they will continue to pose a big threat to safety,  tranquillity and good 
morals the youth and the faithful in residential areas;  
 
Now th erefore, be it resolved by the County A ssembly of Mombasa that  the County 
government of Mombasa to prohibit the operation of bars and night clubs within a 
radius of 300 metres in all residential areas, any institution of basic education 
including nursery, primary, secondary or other learning institutions and places of 
worship in the County;
