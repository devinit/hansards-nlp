NOTICE S OF MOTION  
ISSUANCE OF TITLE DEEDS TO LEARNING INSTITUTIONS AND HEALTHCARE FACILITIES IN MOMBASA 
COUNTY  
 
Yes, Honourable  Bernard Ogutu Ochieng, MCA Changamwe Ward and Chairman Health 
Committee?  
 
Hon. Ogutu:  Thank you Mr. Speaker.  I rise to issue the following notice of Motion;  
 
AWARE THAT  Mombasa County is a home to numerous learning institutions and 
healthcare facilities that play a pivotal role in the development and well -being of our community;  
 
FURTHER AWARE  THAT  a significant number of these institutions despite their 
substantial contribution to our society do not posses valid title deeds for their properties;  
 
COGNIZANT THAT  the absence of title deeds has resulted in encroachments on their 
land, leading to security concerns and hampering their ability to operate efficiently;  
 
NOTING THAT  the Taskforce on Healthcare system established by the Governor has 
highlighted these issues in its report emphasizing the urgent need for action to protect and secure 
essential healthcare facilities;  
 
I, urge this Assembly to RESOLVES  that; 
 
The County government through the departments of Lands, Housing and Urban Planning 
in collaboration with the National Land Commission to ensure that learning institution and 
healthcare facilities within the County are issued with title deeds.  
 
The Temporary Speaker  (Hon. Makanga) : Thank you . Yes Clerk?

Ibrahim Omondi who is out on other official duties. Thank  you, Mr. Speaker , I beg to move the 
following Motion;  
 
THAT AWARE  that level 3 -A healthcare facilities play a critical role in ensuring the right 
to affordable and accessible healthcare for all residents in the County as enshrined in Article 43  
(1) a)  of the Fourth Schedule in Constitution of Kenya, 2010 ; 
 
FURTHER AWARE  that Part 111 of the Mombasa County Health Act, 2018  stipulates the 
duties of County government department for Health in ensuring comprehensive medical services 
to our constituents;  
 
NOTING THAT  level 3 -A healthcare facilities in the County encounter challenges that 
range from inadequate staffing and outdated to insufficient infrastructure and limited access to 
essential medicines and supplies;  
 
COGNIZANT THAT  an efficient and well functioning healthcare system especially at the 
intermediate level is essential in preventing the escalation of common health issues into more 
severe and costly conditions;  
 
CONCERNED  if level 3 -A and other County public health facilities are not well equipped 
the standards health service delivery in the county will be compromised;  
 
I, therefore urge this Assembly to RESOLVE ; 
 
1. That the Department of Health Services in collaboration with relevant stakeholders, 
prioritize the improvement of infrastructure, equipment and facilities at all level 3 -A facilities 
healthcare centers to ensure they meet the required standards for effective service delivery  
 
2. That the County government allocate adequate funding and resources to ensure that level 
3-A facilities are adequately staffed with qualified healthcare professionals and support staff to  
meet the healthcare needs of local population  
 
3. That the Department of Health services to establish a comprehensive plan for the 
consistent and timely supply of essential medicines medical supplies and equipment to all level 
3-A and other county public health facilities in the County  
 
4. That the Department of Health Services institute regular monitoring and evaluation 
mechanisms to assess the performance and functionality of public health facilities and take 
corrective actions where necessary.  
 
Thank you, Mr. Sp eaker.
