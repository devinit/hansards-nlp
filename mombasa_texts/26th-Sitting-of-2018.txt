MOTION  
ENHANCEMENT OF PRE -PRIMARY EDUCATION IN  MOMBASA COUNTY  
 
Hon.  Charles Kitula . 
 
Chief Whip (Hon. Kitula):  
 
(Chief Whip (Hon. Kitula) moved the Motion ) 
 
THAT AWARE that part 2 (2) of t he Fo urth Schedule of the Constitution of Kenya , 2010 
mandates the County g overnment to take the charge of pre -primary education, village 
polytechnics, home craft centers  and children facilities;  
 
FURTHER AWARE that pre-primary education forms the basic fo undation of the entire 
education system;  
 
NOTING THAT many public pre  - primary education centers  in the County are in critical 
conditions and lack basic learning equipment ; 
  
CONCERNED THAT lack of immediate response might jeopardize  early childhood 
educa tion in the C ounty;  
 
I therefore urge this Assembly to RESOLVE  that the County g overnment of Mombasa 
through the Department of Education to;  
 
1. Incorporate  donors and well -wishers in building/renovating all public pre -primary  
education centres in the County ;  
2. Provide learning equipment to all public pre -primary centers ;  
3. Give first priority during employment to teachers currently working at various 
pre-primary education centers in the County .  
Thank you Mr. Speaker  sir may I ask Hon.  Ogutu  to second  my Motio n. 
Hon. Ogutu:  Thank you Mr. Speaker I second.
