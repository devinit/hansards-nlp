PAINTING OF TUSKS ADJACENT TO UHURU GARDENS  
 
       Mr. Speaker Sir, I beg to move the following Motion ; 
       THAT AWARE that the famous decorative elephant tusks along Moi Avenue, Mombasa 
are an important county landmark and part of our historical heritage ; 
       FURTHER AWARE that the decorative elephant tusks were erected way back in 1953 to 
commemorate the crowning of Her Majesty Queen Elizabeth II of United Kingdom who then 
was soverei gn ruler in Kenya;  
       CONCERNED that since their construction the metal plates have been depreciating and 
there is need to paint them regularly to maintain them and preserve this important county and 
national landmark;  
       I therefore urge the Coun ty Assembly to RESOLVE that;  
       The County g overnment to annually paint the landmark decorative tusks on Moi Avenue, 
Mombasa and replace any worn out metal plates to preserve this important piece of our 
County and National heritage.
