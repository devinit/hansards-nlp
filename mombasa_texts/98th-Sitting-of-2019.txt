ILLEGAL DUMPING OF SOLID WAS TE IN AFRICAN LINE I N MIRITINI  
 
Yes, Hon. Shebe Athman ? 
 
Hon. Shebe Athman:  Thank you Mr. Speaker . Mr. Speaker in relation to illegal 
dumping of solid waste in  African Line in Miritini . I think every Hon. Memb er has been given a 
copy of my Statement ; Mr. Speaker , I just want to read the concerns which were raise d by Hon. 
Member of Miritini Ward , my neighbour Hon. Kibwana .  
Mr. Speaker , concerning the issue on dumping solid waste by the County government of 
Mombasa garbage  trucks  in Africa Line in Miri tini. Mr. Speaker it was pointed out that over this 
activity was  likely to result into health an d environmental consequence s in Miritini Ward. 
 Therefor e Mr. Speaker I wish  to report that I visited the s ite together with the CECM for 
Environment, Waste management and energy; his County Chief Officer; and the County Direc tor 
of National Environment Management Authority  (NEMA) to assess the situation. We also held a 
meeting with the affected citizens and agreed on the following;  
 
1. That dumping on the site by County trucks be stopped immediately ; 
2. That the Department levels t he site and cover the waste with soil to prevent open burning 
by scavenging person s and reduce the characteristics smell ; 
3. That the Department of Inspectorate in collaboration with the local community  enforce 
no dumping by any  other private collectors ; 
4. That the Department considers establi shment vegetation at the site ( Tree planting) when 
conditions are favourable.  
 
 Mr. Speaker the current status : 
 
1. Dumping of waste by County trucks was stopped on Friday 25th October, 2019 and  it 
remains in force ; 
2. The Chief Offi cer, Devolution and Public Service Adminis tration was informed to deploy 
Inspectorate officers to control further dumping by private collector’s . 
 
Thank you Mr. Speaker.
