1. INTRODUCTION  
 
       Mr. Speaker Sir and Hon . Members of the County  Assembly, it is wi th great pleasure to 
table the Report  on the vetting of Mr. Hamis i Mwaguya , His Excellency the Governor’s 
nominee for the position of County  Executive Member for Trade, Energy and Investment in 
our County  Government of Mombasa.  The nominee was nominated by His Excellency the 
Governor vide his letter to the County  Assembly Clerk Ref: Msa  CG/ESTAB/4/VOL.I/80 
dated 30th May 2016.  Pursuant to Section 35(1) of the County  Governments Act, 2012 and 
Interim Standing Order No 42 (2) and 195, the nomination stood committed to the Committee 
on Appointments of this Honorable County  Assembly.  Through a press advertisement 
appearing in “The Star” Newspaper on Thursday, 14th July 2016, the nominee and the Public 
were informed of the vetting exercise and the public was invited to send written memoranda 
on the suitability of otherwise of the nominee.   The vetting exercise w as conducted by the 
Committee on Saturday, 16th July 2016 from 10 :00a.m at the Mombasa County  Assembly 
Buildings, Committee Room 1.  This Report  provides the findings and recommendation on the 
suitability for appointment to the position of the nominee for c onsideration by this Honorable 
House and approval of the same.

1. OBSERVATIONS AND FINDINGS  
1.1 The Committee did not receive any adverse Report s, presentations or complaints for 
or against the appointment of Mr. Hamisi Mwaguya for the position of County  
Executive Member for Trade, Energy and Investment in the County  Government of 
Mombasa.  
 
1.2 The Commi ttee was impressed by the exemplary educational achievements and 
experience of Mr. Hamisi Mwaguya and found him to be suitable for appointment to 
the position of County  Executive Member for Trade, Energy and Investment in the 
County  Government of Mombasa.  
 
1.3 The nominee being vetted answered all questions that were asked by the Committee 
satisfactorily and in sufficient detail.  
 
1.4 The nominee has a wide knowledge and experience in Trade and Investment matters 
thus making him suitable for appointment to the posi tion of County  Executive 
Member for Trade, Energy and Investment in the County  Government of Mombasa.  
 
1.5 Mr. Hamisi Mwaguya, the nominee for the position presented the following 
certificates/testimonials to the Committee.  
Educational Background : 
1. Post Graduate  Diploma in Local and Regional Economic Development (Dundee 
University, UK) 2015;  
2. BA (Hons .), Management Studies  (Moi University) 1997;  
3. Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (Chogoria Boys High School) 1991;  
4. Kenya Certificate of Primary Education  (Sawa Sawa Academy) 1987.  
      Other Qualifications : 
1. Certified Public Accountants – Part II  
Appointments : 
1. November 2009 to 2012  - Chairperson: Economic Stimulus Programme Committee  
(Under the Ministry of Finance);  
2. June 2012 to current  - Member, Board of Directo rs (Kenya National Trading 
Corporation);  
3. June 2015 to current  - Member of the Institute of Economic Development.  
1.6 The nominee was asked a number of questions relating to the Department of Trade, 
Energy and Investment to which he replied to same briefly, as follows: -  
(a) On the brief history of himself to which he replied that he was born in Frere  
Town, Mombasa then went to Meru for primary and secondary education.  He 
then went to Moi University, Eldoret.   
       Employed in the Shipping industry for almost ni ne (9) years then joined Mombasa 
County  Government 2013 as Economic Adviser till November 2013 then appointed as 
County  Secretary for almost two (2) years.  In June 2015 he was appointed as Chief of Staff,

the tribunal an d they will look into their plight.  
 
e. On the issue of Chapter Six of Constitution of Kenya to which he replied that he is 
aware and it talks about Leadership and Integrity.  He further said that he is fully 
conversant with Chapter Six of the Constitution of  Kenya, 2010 and he has what it 
takes to be a leader.   
 
f. He informed the Committee that he has never been mentioned adversely in any 
corruption related matter.  
 
2. CONCLUSION  
2.1 The Committee conducted the vetting exercise and was satisfied with the knowledge, 
experience and capacity of Mr. Hamisi Mwaguya to handle the position of County  
Executive Member for Trade, Energy and Investment in the County  Government of 
Mombasa and therefore suitable for appointment to the position.  
 
2.2 The Committee held a meeting on Mon day, 18th July 2016 at 3:30 p.m and adopted 
this Report .
