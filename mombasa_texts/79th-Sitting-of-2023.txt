PROPOSAL TO AMEND PART XXI AND THE SECOND SCHEDULE OF THE MOMBASA COUNTY ASSEMBLY 
STANDING ORDERS  
 
 Honourable  Members , I have received a proposal to amend Part XXI and the Second 
Schedule of the Mombasa County Assem bly Standing Orders from Hon ourable  Athman Rama 
Mwamwiri. Hon ourable Athman Rama Mwamwiri , please proceed  and inform the House of your 
proposal.  
 
 The Leader of the Majority (Hon. Athman Mwamwiri) : Thank you Mr. Speaker  Sir. 
Mr. Speaker Sir , pursuant to Standing Order 233 of the Mombasa County Standing Orders, I 
hereby tender my humble request to the County Assembly Procedure and Rules Committee to 
consider the following amendments to the Standing Orders.  
 
THAT  the County Assembly p erform s most of its functions throu gh Committees. The 
Committees being Housekeeping, w atchdog and Sectoral Committees ; 
  
THAT, these Committees have to be enshrined in specific Standing Orders or in the Second 
Schedule of the Mombasa County Assembly Standing Orders;  
 
THAT, in order for the County Assembly to operate optimally, I propose the following 
amendments to the Mombasa County Assembly Standing Orders;  
 
1. Incorporation of Powers and Privileges Committee which is established pursuant 
to Section 15 of the County Assemblies Power and Privileges  Act, 2017; Section 15 
provides that; ‘‘There is established, for each County Assemb ly, a Committee known as the 
Committee of Powers and Privileges ’’, 
2. THAT, Standing Order 185 be amended by deleting Public Investment so as the 
Committee to be known as  the County Public Accounts Committee , 
3. THAT, a new Standing Order be inserted to establish a Committee to be known as 
the County Public Investment Committee  
4. THAT, a new Standing Order be inserted to establish a ommittee to be known as 
County Security and Disas ter Management Committee  
5. THAT, a new Standing Order be inserted to establish a Committee to be known as 
Equal Opportunities for the purpose of considering the interests of Persons with 
Disabilities and marginalized person within the County.  
6. THAT, the Second Schedule be amended as follows ; 
i. Committee on Agriculture and Livestock  
ii. Committee on Blue Economy and Fisheries  
iii. Committee on Education  
iv. Committee on Information Technology  
v. Committee on Youth, Gender, Sports and So cial Services  
vi. Committee on Trade, Investments and Cooperatives  
vii. Committee on Tourism and Culture  
viii. Committee on Transport and Infrastructure  
ix. Committee on Lands, Housing and Urban Planning  
x. Committee on Environment and Solid Waste Management  
xi.     Committee on Health
