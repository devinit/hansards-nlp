MAINTENANCE  OF SEWER  LINES  BY YOUTH  WITHIN  THE  ESTATES  
 
THAT AWARE , the National Empowerment Authority Act , 2016 provides a legal 
framework for the state to take affirmative measures to ensure youth and marginalized 
groups access employment and economic empowerment;  
 
FURT HER AWARE , that Section 3 (c) of the Act provides a framework to facilitate 
increased employment of Kenya ns in both National and County governments;  
 
CONCERNED THAT,  despite the bulging youth population, job opportunities are scarce 
and lack of jobs to the youth has led to increase in crimes within the estates;  
 
COGNIZANT THAT,  there are jobs within the County such as maintenance of sewer 
lines which can be performed by the youth to reduce unemployment amongst them ; 
 
I therefore urge this Assembly to RESOLVE that:
