Thank you.  
Hon. (Ms.) Boniface : Mr. Speaker Sir I would like to give Notic e of M otion about 
the Decentralization  of the sewerage system in the County. Mr. Speaker  sir I beg t o give 
notice of the following M otion;  
 
(Hon. (Ms.) Boniface moved the Notice of Motion)  
 
THAT AWARE  that the County g overnment is mandated with responsibil ity of 
County health services, including in particular refuse removal, refuse dumps and 
solid waste disposal under Part 2(g) of Fourth  Schedule of the Constitution  of 
Kenya 2010 ; 
 
FURTHER AWARE that the sewerage system in the County was constructed in the 
colonial  era and has been concentrated in few areas of island;  
 
NOTING THAT sewerage systems and general sanitation has always been 
coordinated from the County level;  
 
FURTHER NOTING that the existing infrastructure is inadequate and dilapidated 
and cannot  adequately cater for ever increasing demand for the service;  
 
CONCERNED that the current state of sewers in the County may pose a threat to 
both the residents and the environment in the County ; 
  
I therefore urge this Assembly to resolve that:  
 
1. The County  government construct and decentralise the sewerage system to all the 
Wards in the County.  
 
2. County g overnment transfer sewerage systems management to W ard level .  
Thank you , Mr. Speaker .
