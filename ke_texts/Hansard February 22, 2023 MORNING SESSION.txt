Energy and Natural Resources  which should be able to report back to this House  within sixty  
days. The Chairperson  please take note. Next order  
NOTICE OF MOTION  
CASHLESS MODE OF PAYMENT IN ALL THE RELEVANT HEALTH FACILITIES  
The Speaker ( Hon. Joel Karuri): There is a Notice of Motion from the Member  from 
Keringet Ward  
Hon. William Tonui : Honorable Speaker I want to move a  Notice of Motion that, that whereas 
County Health Facilities offer range of services to visiting Patient s across the County; Noting 
that patients are charged varied amounts at Levels Four and Five Hospitals depending on the 
services offered; Appreciatin g that these facilities relies on the revenue generated from the  
hospital to improve the services; Cognizant  that most Level Four and Five Hospitals prefer 
cash systems as a convenient mode of payment; Appreciating  that the government is now 
embracing cashless  systems  in rendering most of its services to make transacti ons more 
efficient, minimize risk of corruption and improving accountability;  This House resolves that 
the County Government through the department of  Finance to come up with appropriate and 
convenient Cashless Mode of payment in all the relevant Health Facilities to improve financials 
accountability. Honorable  Members I beg to give the notice of  Motion .
