 COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF NAKURU  
THE HANSARD  
Tuesday, 1 5TH November  2022  
Assembly Building  
The House met at 10:30 am 
[The Deputy Speaker (Hon. Joseph Ng ’ang’a ) in the Chair].  
PRAYER  
COMMUNICATION FROM THE CHAIR  
THANKS TO MEMBERS  
The Deputy Speaker (Hon. Joseph Ng ’ang’a ): Hon. Members, I want to take this opportunity to 
thank all of you for having elected me as your Deputy Speaker. I will do everything to protect the 
integrity and the interest of the Members of this House .  
PETITION  
UTILIZATION OF BURSARY FUNDS  
The Deputy Speaker (Hon. Joseph Ng ’ang’a ): We have a Petition by the Nakuru Residents , 
founders of Grassroots Civilian  Oversight Initiative concerning the utilization of bursary funds. 
This stand  committed to the Committee on Education .  
PAPER  
AUDITOR GENERAL REPORT ON NAKURU COUNTY BURSARY FUND  
FOR THE YEAR ENDED 30TH JUNE 2021  
The Deputy Speaker (Hon. Joseph Ng ’ang’a ): We have a Paper to be laid by the Leader of 
Majority.  
The Leader of Majority (Hon. Alex Lang’at ): Thank you Mr. Deputy Speaker sir. on Order 
number 5, the report of the Auditor General on Nakuru County Bursary Funds for the year which 
ended 30th June, 2021. I wish to lay the report.  
                                            (The Report was laid on the table)  
The Deputy Speaker (Hon. Joseph Ng ’ang’a ): Thank you Majority Leader . That report stands 
committed to the Committee on Public Accounts an d Investment.

MOTION  
THE HOUSE RESOLVE TO ADJOURN FROM 15TH NOVEMBER , 2022  TO 22ND NOVEMBER , 2022  
The Deputy Speaker (Hon. Joseph Ng ’ang’a ): We have a Mot ion to be moved  by the Leader of 
Majority.  
The Leader of Majority (Hon. Alex Lang’at ): Thank you Mr. Deputy Speaker sir. Pursuant to 
the provisions of Standing Order 31(4), I beg to move a Motion ; 
THAT the House  resolve s to adjourn from today, 15th November, 2022 and that the House resumes 
its normal business next week on 22nd November , 2022 .  
This is because we have a lot of pending issues  and other engagements that we need to tackle so 
that the whole House can continue operatin g normally. I beg to move this Motion and request for 
someone to second. Thank you.  
The Deputy Speaker (Hon. Joseph Ng ’ang’a ): Thank you. Any seconder?  
Hon. Alex Mbugua: Thank you Mr. Deputy Speaker sir. I beg to second.  
The Deputy Speaker (Hon. Joseph Ng ’ang’a ): Thank you Hon. Alex for seconding.  
Hon. Peter Palang’a: Thank you Mr. Deputy Speaker sir. I think we are all aware of other 
engagements that these Hon. Members are supposed to be handling. Without preempting, I think 
all Members are aware of other schedules and engagements . I would wish with your indulgence 
that you put to question this matter.  
(Question p roposed  that pursuant to provision of section 31(4), the House resolves to adjo urn 
from 15th November, 2022 and resumes on 22nd November, 2022 ) 
(Question put and agreed to)
